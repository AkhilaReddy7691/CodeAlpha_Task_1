My proposed project:-

My project name is HOSTEL SEARCH APP.A hostel search app provides a valuable tool for travelers seeking affordable and comfortable accommodations. The app includes location-based search functionality that uses GPS to locate nearby hostels on a map. Users can view detailed information about each hostel, including photos, reviews, amenities, and pricing. The app allows users to book a hostel room directly from the app, including selecting the number of beds, dates, and other preferences. Additionally, users can filter their search based on price, location, amenities, and other criteria. The app provides personalized recommendations based on a user's travel history, interests, and other factors. Users can also receive notifications about new nearby hostels, promotions, and when a booked hostel room is ready for check-in. With features such as location-based search, booking functionality, and personalized recommendations, a hostel search app simplifies the process of finding and booking a hostel room for travelers.
